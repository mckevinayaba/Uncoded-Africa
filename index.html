About Uncoded Africa

South Africa unemployment

Africa produces 10–12 million graduates every year, but only 3 million jobs are created. Youth unemployment in many countries exceeds 60%. In South Africa, where Uncoded Africa launches, the unemployment rate is 32.9%, with youth unemployment at 62.4%.

Millions of talented Africans remain without opportunities, not because they lack ambition, but because traditional systems cannot absorb them. Many believe they cannot start businesses because they lack coding skills, advanced degrees, or technical training.

Uncoded Africa exists for: Students and dropouts • Job seekers and career changers • People stuck in jobs they don't love • Anyone who wants to solve Africa's challenges but doesn't know where to start.

This is not another conference. It's a 7-day transformation journey where you build live, guided by people who have done it before.

7-Day Transformation Journey

From curiosity to creation — where every session ends with something live

Day 1–2 (Mon–Tue Mar 30–31)

Build Camp

Launch your first project by sunset. Master the ecosystem of no-code and AI-first tools: Webflow, Bubble, Glide, Softr, Airtable, Make, Zapier, Replit, Cursor, Lovable, Base44, Bolt, Gemini, and more emerging platforms.

  • • Build your first live page
  • • Experience instant wins with hands-on guidance
Ship something live • Get your first win
Day 3 (Wed Apr 1)

AI Helpers

AI for content, data, and smart agents. Use AI to write, organize, and automate.

Vibe Coding Workshop (14:00–17:00)

Turn your idea into working software using AI-powered code generation. Speak or type in plain language.

Vibe Coding Party (19:00–22:00)

Live demos, music, and open mic showcases where AI builds on the big screen.

AI appears as helper, not hurdle
Day 4 (Thu Apr 2)

Marketing & Sales

From idea to income — AI-driven growth strategies.

  • • AI Marketing: Automate content, email campaigns, social media, and ads
  • • Sales Automation: WhatsApp, SMS, and payment integration
  • • Go-to-Market in 24 Hours: Build funnels, test ads, launch live
  • • Pricing Mastery: Use AI to model, test, and optimize pricing
Day 5 (Fri Apr 3)

Growth & Scaling

Learn how to scale what you've built.

  • • Investor Readiness: Pitch with AI-powered storytelling and decks
  • • Analytics & Growth Loops: Use AI dashboards to track KPIs
  • • Collaboration Tools: Integrate with Slack, Notion, Trello, and more
  • • Funding Pathways: Meet VCs, angels, and ecosystem funders
Day 6 (Sat Apr 4)

Impact & Ecosystem

Build for Africa, build for good.

  • • SMME & SME Focus: Tools for small businesses to digitize fast
  • • Sector Use Cases: Health, agriculture, logistics, education
  • • Innovation for Good: Climate, resilience, and inclusion
  • • Ambitious & Hungry Builders: Space for non-developers to turn grit into growth
Day 7 (Sun Apr 5)

Demo Day & Awards

Showcase and celebrate.

  • • Demo Day: Live pitches and product showcases to investors, corporates, and peers
  • • Uncoded Africa Awards: Celebrate innovators in 16+ categories
  • • Networking Gala: Connect with builders, sponsors, and global ecosystem leaders
Leave with a live product, a network, and a new identity as a builder
